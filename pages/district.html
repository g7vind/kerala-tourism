THRISSUR

Thrissur, often called the cultural capital of Kerala, India, is renowned for its vibrant festivals, especially the grand Thrissur Pooram. The city boasts ancient temples, churches, and traditional architecture. With a rich history and cultural heritage, Thrissur is a captivating blend of tradition and modernity, drawing visitors with its unique charm

ERNAKULAM

Ernakulam, located in the Indian state of Kerala, is a bustling commercial and financial hub known for its vibrant culture and historical significance. The city, also known as Kochi, is characterized by a blend of modern infrastructure and traditional heritage, featuring landmarks like Fort Kochi, Mattancherry Palace, and vibrant markets.

THIRUVANANTHAPURAM

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, India, is known for its rich cultural heritage and historic landmarks. Nestled along the Arabian Sea, it offers a blend of traditional and modern elements. Attractions include the Padmanabhaswamy Temple, museums, lush green landscapes, and a vibrant arts scene

WAYANAD

Wayanad, nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India, is a picturesque district known for its lush green landscapes, dense forests, and diverse wildlife. Renowned for its spice plantations, tea estates, and pristine natural beauty, Wayanad offers a tranquil retreat with attractions like Chembra Peak, Banasura Sagar Dam, and Edakkal Caves.

KOZHIKODE

Kozhikode, located on the southwest coast of India, is a historic city in Kerala. Known for its rich cultural heritage and spice trade history, Kozhikode offers scenic beaches, vibrant markets, and significant landmarks like the historic Pazhassiraja Museum. The city blends tradition with modernity, making it a diverse and captivating destination.

IDUKKI

Idukki is a picturesque district located in the Indian state of Kerala. Nestled in the Western Ghats, it is known for its lush green landscapes, diverse flora, and fauna. Famous for the Idukki Dam, one of the largest arch dams in Asia, the region attracts nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The district offers a serene retreat with its tea plantations.
